Targeted Exercise Programs

Chest Workouts

Includes bench press, incline press, and push-ups to build a wide and strong chest.

Leg Workouts

Squats, lunges, and leg presses improve lower body strength and balance.

Back Workouts

Shoulder Workouts

Recommended Diet Plans

For Chest

High protein (chicken breast, eggs), moderate carbs (brown rice), healthy fats (nuts).

For Legs

Complex carbs (oats, quinoa), lean protein (fish, lentils), iron-rich veggies (spinach).

For Back

Omega-3s (salmon, chia), whey protein, antioxidant fruits (berries, banana).

For Shoulders

Lean meats, tofu, vitamin C (oranges), and healthy oils for joint strength.
